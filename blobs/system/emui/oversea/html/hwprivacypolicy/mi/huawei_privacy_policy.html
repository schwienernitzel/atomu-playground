Tauākī Tūmataitinga Pakihi Kaihokohoko a Huawei

He mea urutau te Tauākī Tūmataitinga Pakihi Kaihokohoko a Huawei i te

Paengawhāwhā 15, 2018.

Ka manaaki a Huawei Device (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. me ana hoa kamupene ā-ao (whakatōpū, "Huawei", "mātou", "tātou", me "tō mātou") i tō tūmataitinga. Nō reira, kua waihangatia e mātou he Tauākī Tūmataitinga (ka kīia mai i konei ko "tēnei Tauākī") e kapi ana i te āhua o tā mātou kohi, whakamahi, whāki, tiaki, rokiroki me te whakawhiti i ō raraunga whaiaro. Me whai wā koe ki te pānui me te mōhio ki tā mātou tauākī tūmataiti me te whakamōhio mai mēnā he pātai āu.


Ko ngā raraunga whaiaro ko ngā mōhiohio hiko, ētahi atu rānei mā ēnei anake, tāpiri ki ētahi atu mōhiohio rānei ka taea te whakamahi hei tautuhi i tētahi tangata, e mōhiotia rānei ia. Ka whakamārama tēnei Tauākī i te āhua o te tukatuka a Huawei i ō raraunga whaiaro, engari kāore e kapi ana ngā āhuatanga tukatuka katoa i te mea ka whakamōhiotia koe ki ngā mahinga tukatuka whāiti. Nō reira, i mua i te whakamahi i tētahi hua whāiti, ratonga rānei, e tūtohua ana kia pānuitia e koe te pānui tūmataitinga, tauākī tāpiri rānei i whakaputaina e Huawei mō taua hua, ratonga rānei kia mōhio ai he pēhea te tukatuka i ō raraunga whaiaro.

E hāngai ana tēnei Tauākī ki ngā hua whaiaro Huawei me te kāinga anake, tae atu ki ngā waea āhuahira, waea atamai, paparorohiko, pirerorohiko, pūrere whakamau, pūrere aunui pūkoro, pūrerenui ā-whare atamai, apatoko, taupānga rorohiko, ratonga pūkoro, pūmanawa, keteutauta, paetukutuku, ratonga hoki e whakaatu ana, whakahua rānei i tēnei Tauākī.

E whakaahua ana tēnei Tauākī:

2. He Pēhea te Whakamahi a Huawei i Ngā Pihikete me Ngā Hangarau Ōrite

I. He Pēhea te Kohikohi me te Whakamahi a Huawei i Ō Raraunga Whaiaro

A. Ngā Raraunga Whaiaro ka Kohia e Huawei

I mua i te whakamahi i ngā hua Huawei, ratonga rānei a Huawei, me tuku mai pea e koe ngā raraunga whaiaro. Ehara i te mea me tuku e koe ō raraunga whaiaro ki Huawei, engari i ētahi āhuatanga, nā te kore o te tuku i ētahi raraunga whaiaro kāore e taea ētahi hua hāngai, ratonga rānei te tuku atu ki a koe.


Ka kohia e Huawei me te tukatuka i ō raraunga whaiaro mō te kaupapa kua kīia i roto i tēnei Tauākī. Anei ētahi tauira o ngā raraunga whaiaro ka kohia pea e mātou:

1. Ngā raraunga whaiaro ka tukuna e koe ki Huawei

Me rēhita koe rēhita koe i tētahi Huawei ID hei whakamahi i ētahi taumahi whāiti, ratonga rānei. Ina rēhita koe i tētahi Huawei ID, te takiuru rānei mā tētahi Huawei ID hei hokohoko tuihono, te tikiake pūmanawa, te hoko ratonga rānei, ka tonoa koe ki te tuku mai i ngā raraunga whaiaro hāngai, pērā i tō ingoa, wāhitau īmēra, tau waea pūkoro, mōhiohio ota, wāhitau utanga, aratau utunga.

Ka whakaratoa pea ngā ratonga rokiroki kapua ki a koe e Huawei e taea e koe te tukutahi me te pūrua i ētahi o ō kōnae, pikitia, raraunga hoki.

Mā ētahi hua Huawei ka taea e koe te whakawhitiwhiti me te tiri mōhiohio me ētahi atu. Ina whakamahia e koe tētahi hua Huawei hei tiri mea me tō whānau, hoa hoki, me waihanga pea e koe he Huawei ID e whai ana i ngā mōhiohio whāiti ka wātea tūmatanui, tae atu ki tētahi ingoa kārangaranga, awata hoki. Ka kohia e koe e Huawei ngā mōhiohio e hāngai ana ki aua tāngata, pērā i ō rātau ingoa, wāhitau īmēra, tau waea pūkoro hoki. Ka whai a Huawei i ngā tikanga tōtika, waiwai hoki hei whakarite i te haumarutanga o ngā mōhiohio e tukuna ana e koe. Me mātua whakarite kua whakaae ngā tāngata, nā rātou ngā mōhiohio e tukuna ana e koe, kia tukuna e koe ā rātau mōhiohio ki a Huawei.

Hei whakatutuki i ngā whakaritenga o ētahi takiwā ture mō ngā whakamotuhēhēnga ingoa tūturu o ngā pūkete, te ārai i ngā waranga kēmu ā-hiko, utunga Ipurangi, ka tonoa pea koe e Huawei kia tuku whakaaturanga tuakiri koe mai i te kāwanatanga, he kāri mōhiohio hāngai rānei e taea ai koe te tāutu.

2. Ngā mōhiohio ka kohia e Huawei i roto i tō whakamahi i ngā ratonga

Ka kohia e Huawei ngā raraunga mō tō pūrere me te āhua o tō whakawhiti me te whakawhiti a tō pūrere i ngā mōhiohio me ngā hua, ratonga hoki a Huawei. Kei roto i tēnei momo mōhiohio ko:

(1) Ngā mōhiohio pūrere me te taupānga, pērā i te ingoa pūrere, waehere tāutunga pūrere (IMEI, ESN, MEID, me SN), wā whakahohe pūrere, tauira pūmārō, putanga OS, putanga taupānga, waehere tāutunga pūmanawa, me ngā tautuhinga pūrere me te taupānga (pērā i te rohe, reo, rohe wā, rahi momotuhi).

(2) Ngā mōhiohio whatunga pūkoro, pērā i te TT kaiwhakarato whatunga waea pūkoro whenua tūmatanui (PLMN) me te wāhitau Kawa Ipurangi (KI).

(3) Ngā mōhiohio rangitahi. Ina whakamahia e koe ngā ratonga a Huawei, te tiro rānei i ngā ihirangi a Huawei, ka kohia aunoatia e Huawei me te rangitahi i ētahi mōhiohio, pērā i te wā urunga, tatau urunga, wāhitau KI, me ngā mōhiohio mō ngā āhuatanga (pērā i ngā hapa, hinganga, tīmata anō, whakahōu rānei).

(4) Ngā mōhiohio tauwāhi. Ka kohia, whakamahi, tukatuka hoki e Huawei te tauwāhi āwhiwhi, tika rānei o tō pūrere ina uru atu koe ki ētahi ratonga ā-tauwāhi (hei taura, ina rapu, whakamahi pūmanawa whakaterenga, tiro rānei i te huarere o tētahi tauwāhi whāiti). Ka taea ngā mōhiohio tauwāhi te tiki atu e ai ki te GPS, Wi-Fi, me te TT whatunga kaiwhakarato ratonga. Ka tonoa koe ki te kōwhiri i ngā taupānga e hiahia ana koe ki te whakahohe i ngā ratonga tauwāhi. I te tahua tautuhinga pūrere, ka taea e koe ngā whakaaetanga tauwāhi o ngā ratonga whāiti te mono hei whakakore i ō mōhiohio tauwāhi.

(5) Ngā mōhiohio e tiakina ana e koe ki ngā tūmau Huawei. Hei tauira, ko ngā mōhiohio ka tukuatu e koe ki te kapua ka rokirokitia ki ngā tūmau Huawei mō te uru tere me te tiri waenga pūrere. Kāore e tirohia e mātou ngā mōhiohio ka rokirokitia e koe ki ngā tūmau Huawei.

3. Ngā mōhiohio mai i ngā pūtake rōpū kē

Ina whakaaetia e te ture, ka kohia e Huawei ngā mōhiohio mōu mai i ngā pūtake tūmatanui, arumoni hoki. Ka tīkina anō pea e Huawei ngā mōhiohio whāiti mai i ngā ratonga whatunga pāpori rōpū kē, pērā i te wā ka whakamahia e koe te pūkete whatunga pāpori hei takiuru ki tētahi paetukutuku a Huawei.

4. Te kohikohi me te whakamahi i ngā raraunga tāutu-kore

E kōrero ana ngā raraunga tāutu-kore mō ngā raraunga e kore e taea te whakamahi hei tāutu i te tangata. Ko ngā tauira o ngā raraunga tāutu-kore ko ngā tatauranga o ngā toronga paetukutuku, ngā tikiake taupānga, rōrahi hokohoko hua. Ka kohia e Huawei ngā mōhiohio tatauranga kai mōhio ai he pēhea te whakamahi a ngā kaiwhakamahi i ā mātou hua me ngā ratonga. Mā te pēnei, ka taea e mātou ā mātou ratonga te whakapai ake kia ū ai ki ō whakaritenga.

Ka wehea motuhaketia e mātou o raraunga whaiaro me ngā raraunga tāutu-kore, ā, ka whakamahia motuhaketia. Ka puta ake pea ngā āhuatanga ina kohia, whakamahi, whakaatu, whakawhiti hoki a Huawei i ngā raraunga tāutu-kore mō ētahi atu kaupapa e ai ki ō mātou whakaaro.


B. He Pēhea te Whakamahi a Huawei i Ō Raraunga Whaiaro

Ka whakamahia pea e Huawei ō raraunga whaiaro mō ngā kaupapa e whai ake:

(1) Te rēhita me te whakahohe hua whaiaro, kāinga hoki a Huawei kua hokona mai e koe.

(2) Te rēhita i tētahi Huawei ID kia pai ai tō whakamahi i ngā taumahi whānui me ngā ratonga waea pūkoro.

(3) Te tuku, whakahohe, manatoko rānei i ngā hua me ngā ratonga kua tonoa e koe, te huri, te whakarato tautoko hangarau rānei me ngā ratonga i muri hokonga mō ngā hua kua kīia i mua me ngā ratonga e ai ki ō whakaritenga.

(4) Te tuku OS, whakahōu taupānga rānei me ngā whakamōhiotanga tāutanga.

(5) Te whakarato wheako kaiwhakamahi whaiaro me ngā ihirangi.

(6) Te tuku mōhiohio ki a koe mō ngā hua me ngā ratonga ka hiahia pea koe, te pōwhiri i a koe ki ngā mahinga hāpai i a Huawei me ngā rangahau mākete, te tuku mōhiohio mākete ki a koe. Ki te kore koe e hiahia ki te tuku i aua mōhiohio, ka taea e koe te putaatu ina hiahia ana koe.

(7) Te whakahaere i ngā ōtita ā-roto, tātari raraunga, me te rangahau; te tātari i te tōtika o te whakahaere pakihi me te ine i ngā hea mākete; me te whakapai ake i ngā hua me ngā ratonga a Huawei.

(8) Te tukutahi, tiri me te rokiroki i ngā raraunga kua tukuatu koe, tikiake rānei, me ngā raraunga e hiahiatia ana mō te tukuatu me te tikiake whakahaere.

(9) Te whakapai ake i tā mātou ārai ngaronga me ngā papatono ārai-tinihanga.

(10) Te tukatuka e ai ki ngā ture me ngā waeture, hei tauira, tāke, ngā tono mana.

(11) Ētahi atu kaupapa i roto i ngā ratonga whāiti, me tō whakaaetanga rānei.


II. He Pēhea te Whakamahi a Huawei i Ngā Pihikete me Ngā Hangarau Ōrite

A. Pihikete

He kōnae kuputuhi tētahi pihikete ka rokirokitia e tētahi tūmau tukutuku i runga i tētahi rorohiko, pūrere pūkoro rānei, me ngā ihirangi o tētahi pihikete ka taea te tiki atu me te pānui ake mā te tūmau i waihanga i te pihikete. He mea ahurei ngā pihikete ki te pūtirotiro, taupānga pūkoro rānei e whakamahia ana e koe. Ko ngā kuputuhi kei roto i tētahi pihikete he whai pūtāutu, ingoa pae, me ētahi tau, pūāhua hoki.

I ētahi wā ka rokirokitia e Huawei ngā pihikete ki ngā rorohiko, pūrere pūkoro rānei mō te kaupapa o te wheako kaiwhakamahi, tae atu ki ngā wāhanga e whai ake:

(1) Ngā pihikete hangarau: Takiuru me te manatokonga. Ina whakamahia e koe he Huawei ID hei takiuru ki tētahi paetukutuku, ka whakarite ngā pihikete "ā-wāhanga" ka tino ngāwari noa ō toronga ki tēnei pae. .

(2) Ngā pihikete whakawhaiaro: Te rokiroki i ō manakohanga me ngā tautuhinga. Ka taea e tētahi paetukutuku ngā pihikete te whakamahi hei tiaki tautuhinga, pērā i te tautuhinga reo me te rahi momotuhi i tō rorohiko, pūrere pūkoro, ngā tuemi kei tō kāta hokohoko, me ētahi atu manakohanga pūtirotiro.

(3) Ngā pihikete pānuitanga. Ka whakamahia e Huawei ngā pihikete hei kohikohi mōhiohio mō ō mahinga tuihono me ngā kaingākau me te whakarato ki a koe ngā pānuitanga e tino hāngai ana ki a koe.

(4) Ngā pihikete tatauranga. Mā ngā pihikete, ka taea e Huawei ngā mōhiohio mōu te kohikohi mō tō whakamahi i ā mātou paetukutuku me ētahi atu taupānga, hei toronga kotahi (e whakamahi ana i tētahi pihikete wāhanga), ngā toronga tāruarua rānei (mā tētahi pihikete mānawanawa rānei).

(5) Ngā Pihikete Pāpāho Pāpori. E tūhonoa ana ngā pihikete pāpāho pāpori ki ngā ratonga e whakaratoa ana e ngā rōpū kē, pērā i ngā pātene 'Painga' me te 'Tiri'. Ka whakaratoa e ngā rōpū kē ēnei ratonga kia āhukahukatia ai i toroa e koe ā mātou paetukutuku.

Ka taea e koe ngā pihikete te whakahaere, muku rānei ina hiahia koe. Mō ngā taipitopito, haere ki AboutCookies.org. Ka taea e koe ngā pihikete katoa te muku e rokirokitia ana i tō rorohiko, ā, ka whakarato kōwhiringa te nuinga o ngā pūtirotiro tukutuku e taea ai te ārai pihikete. Engari, ki te āraia ngā pihikete me huri e koe ō tautuhinga kaiwhakamahi i ia wā e toroa ana e koe tā mātou paetukutuku. Rapu me pēhea te whakahaere i ngā tautuhinga pihikete mō tō pūtirotiro i konei: Internet Explorer > Google Chrome > Mozilla Firefox > Safari > Opera.

Ki te ūkuia e koe ngā pihikete, me uri koe i ō tautuhinga i tō toronga atu anō ki ngā paetukutuku a Huawei. Me mōhio koe, e herea ana e ētahi ratonga Huawei kia whakamahia ngā pihikete. Ki te monokia ngā pihikete ka whai pānga pea ki tō whakamahi i ētahi taumahi, te katoa o ēnei ratonga.

B. Ngā Tukutohu Tukutuku me Ngā Tūtohu Pika

I tua atu i ngā pihikete, ka whakamahia anō pea e Huawei me ētahi rōpū kē ngā tukutohu tukutuku me ngā tūtohu pika kei ngā paetukutuku. Ko te tikanga he atahanga whakairoiro te tohutuku tukutuku kua tāmaua ki tētahi paetukutuku, īmēra rānei hei tāutu i ō pihikete pūrere ina pūtirotiro koe i te paetukutuku, īmēra rānei. Mā ngā tūtohu pika ka āhei ngā Huawei ki te tuku īmēra e taea ai e koe te pānui me te kite hoki mēnā kua huakina te īmēra.

Ka whakamahia e Huawei me ētahi rōpū kē ēnei hangarau mō ngā kaupapa rerekē, tae atu ki ngā whakamahinga ratonga tātaritanga (me ngā pihikete) me te whakarato i ngā ihirangi me ngā pānuitanga e rata ake ana ki a koe. Hei tauira, ina whiwhi koe i tētahi īmēra mai i a Huawei, kei roto pea tētahi URL ā-pāwhiri e tūhono ana i a koe ki tētahi whārangi tukutuku a Huawei. Ki te pāwhiria e koe te hono, ka aroturukihia e Huawei tō toronga kia mōhio ai mātou ki ō manakohanga mō ngā hua, ratonga hoki me te whakapai ake i tā mātou ratonga kiritaki. Ka taea e koe te weteohauru mai i te rārangi mēra a Huawei ina hiahia ana koe mēnā kāore koe i te hiahia kia pēnei te aroturuki i a koe.

C. Ētahi Atu Rokiroki Paetata

Ka whakamahia pea e Huawei me ētahi rōpū ētahi atu hangarau rokiroki paetata, hei tauira, ngā ahanoa tūhono paetata (e mōhiotia anō ko "ngā pihikete Kohiko") me te rokiroki paetata HTML5, i ētahi hua me ngā ratonga whāiti. E ōrite ana ki ngā pihikete, ka rokirokitia ēnei hangarau ngā mōhiohio ki tō pūrere, ā, me te hopu i ētahi mōhiohio mō ō mahinga, manakohanga hoki. Engari, ka whakamahia pea e ēnei hangarau ngā pāpāho rerekē mai i ngā pihikete. Nō reira, kāore pea e taea e koe ēnei te whakahaere mā te whakamahi i ngā utauta pūtirotiro arowhānui me ngā tautuhinga. Mō ētahi atu taipitopito me pēhea te mono, te muku rānei i ngā mōhiohio kei ngā pihikete Kohiko, pāwhiri i konei.

D. Kaua E Aroturuki

He maha ngā pūtirotiro tukutuku he whakarato i te taumahi Kaua E Aroturuki e taea ai te tuku i ngā tono Kaua E Aroturuki ki ngā paetukutuku. I tēnei wā, kāore anō kia whakatūhia e ngā whakahaere whakariterite Ipurangi matua ngā kaupapahere hei tauwhāiti me pēhea te whakarite a ngā paetukutuku i ēnei tono. Ki te whakahohea e koe te Kaua E Aroturuki i tō pūtirotiro, ka whakautehia e ngā paetukutuku katoa a Huawei i tō tīpakonga.


III. He Pēhea te Tiri a Huawei i Ō Raraunga Whaiaro

E kore rawa e tiria e mātou ngā raraunga whaiaro me ētahi atu kamupene, whakahaere, tāngata rānei engari rawa mēnā ka ū ki tētahi o ēnei āhuatanga:

(1) I whakaaehia te tiri: I muri i te whiwhi i tō whakaaetanga, ka tiria e Huawei ngā mōhiohio i whakamanahia e koe ki ngā rōpū kē tauwhāiti, kāwai rānei a ngā rōpū kē.

(2) Te tiri e ai ki ngā ture me ngā waeture: Ka tiria pea e Huawei ō mōhiohio e ai ki ngā ture me ngā waeture, mō te whakatau wenewene ture, e herea rānei e ngā mana whakahaere, ā-ture rānei e ai ki te ture.

(3) Te tiri ki ngā hoa kamupene o Huawei: Ka tiria pea ō mōhiohio ki roto i ngā hoa kamupene o Huawei mō ngā kaupapa whāiti, tika anake, ā, ka tiria anake ko ngā mōhiohio e hiahiatia ana e ngā ratonga. Hei tauira, ka manatokona e mātou te ahureitanga ā-ao o ngā pūkete i mau i te whakaaetanga kia rēhitatia.

(4) Te tiri ki ngā hoa pakihi: Ka whakaratoa e ētahi hua, ratonga hoki/rānei hāngai ki a koe mai i o mātou hoa pakihi. Ka tiria anō pea e Huawei ō mōhiohio ki a rātou, ā, ka whakamahia pea ō mōhiohio hei whakarato hua, ratonga hoki/rānei ka tonoa e koe (hei tauira, ngā hua ka hokona e ngā kaihoko rōpū kē mā te pūhara ī-ōhanga a Huaweingā ihirangi ataata i whakaratoa e ētahi atu kamupene mā ngā taupānga a Huawei), te tuku matapae mō ō kaingākau me te whakarato pea i ngā pānuitanga, rauemi whakatairanga me ētahi atu rauemi pea ki a koe.

(5) Te tiri ki ngā kaiwhakarato ratonga: Ka whāki anō pea a Huawei i ō mōhiohio ki ngā kamupene whakarato ratonga mā mātou. Ko ngā tauira o ēnei kaiwhakarato ratonga ko ngā kamupene whakarato ratonga waeawera, tuku īmēra, whakarato tautoko hangarau rānei mā Huawei. Ka taea e ngā kaiwhakarato ratonga ō mōhiohio te whakarato mō ngā kaupapa whakarato ratonga anake ki a koe mā Huawei.

(6) Ka tiria e Huawei ō mōhiohio ina tika ana kia pēnei, hei tauira, kia ū ki tētahi ture hāngai, waeture, tukanga ture, nā te kāwanatanga rānei.

I ngā wāhanga 3 ki te 6, ka whakarite a Huawei ka ū tēnei tiringa ki te ture me te waitohu i ngā whakaaetanga whāki-kore (NDA) pākaha, ngā whiti tukatuka raraunga hoki/rānei me ngā kamupene, whakahaere, tāngata hoki me te hunga e tiria ana ngā raraunga whaiaro, e here ana i a rātou kia ū ki tēnei Tauākī me te whakarite i ngā tikanga matatapu me te haumarutanga tōtika ina tukatuka ana i ngā raraunga whaiaro.


IV. He Pēhea te Tiaki a Huawei i Ō Raraunga Whaiaro

He mea nui rawa ki a Huawei te haumarutanga o ō raraunga whaiaro, ā, kua whakamanahia e ngā tikanga ahumahi arowhānui hei tiaki i ō raraunga whaiaro me te ārai kia kaua e uru pokanoa, whāki, whakamahi, whakakē, tūkino, ngaro rānei. Nā tēnei, ka whai a Huawei i ēnei whakaritenga:
